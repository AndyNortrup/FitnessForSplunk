To join Fitness for Splunk, you must have a fitness
tracking device and an associated Google Fit, Microsoft
HealthCloud, or Fitbit account.


To continue, simply select your account type and
authorize Fitness for Splunk to access and display
certain fitness account information, such as your:


  • Name (or user ID), age and gender
  • Current or recent fitness activity
  • Fitness goals
  • Fitness tracking account type
